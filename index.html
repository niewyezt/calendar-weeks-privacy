Privacy Policy

App: GLOBAL DAYS OFF & EVENTS
Effective date: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GLOBAL DAYS OFF & EVENTS (“the App”) handles information. The App is designed to work primarily on the user’s device. Depending on which features you use, some data related to purchases, subscriptions, and app functionality may also be processed by third-party providers such as Google Play and RevenueCat.

In plain English:
The developer does not run a custom backend for ordinary app use. Most app settings and local content stay on your device. If you use premium features, subscription and purchase-related data may be processed by Google Play and RevenueCat to handle billing, entitlement validation, restore purchases, and related functionality.

1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App may store certain information locally on your device, such as:

  • app settings and preferences,
  • selected country, language, holiday, and display options,
  • locally cached app data,
  • other user-created or app-generated content that is intended to remain on-device.

This locally stored information is generally not transmitted to the developer’s own servers.

2. Location Data

The App may access your device’s location only when you choose to use location-based functionality related to sunrise and sunset calculations.

Location access is optional. You can allow or deny location permission, and you can continue using the App without location access, although sunrise and sunset functionality may then be limited or unavailable.

The developer does not use a custom backend to store your location data from ordinary use of the App. Location data used for sunrise and sunset functionality is intended to remain on your device and is not transmitted to the developer’s own servers as part of normal app use.

Manual selections such as country, language, holidays, and other preferences may be stored locally on your device as part of normal app settings.

3. Purchases and Subscriptions

If you use paid features, free trials, subscriptions, or purchase restoration features, certain purchase-related information may be processed by third-party providers, including Google Play and RevenueCat.

This may include, for example:

  • purchase history,
  • subscription status,
  • entitlement status,
  • technical identifiers needed to validate or restore purchases,
  • basic technical information required for app functionality, fraud prevention, and service reliability.

Payment card details and payment transaction handling are processed directly by Google Play or its payment partners, not by the developer through the App.

If you use paid features, purchase-related information may be processed by Google Play and RevenueCat to handle billing, subscription status, entitlement validation, restore purchases, premium access management, service reliability, and limited subscription analytics.

Certain transaction-related or subscription-related information may be retained where necessary to comply with legal, tax, accounting, fraud-prevention, chargeback, or claims-defense obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party service providers to enable certain features. In particular:

  • Google Play may process payment, billing, subscription, and purchase-related information.
  • RevenueCat may process subscription and entitlement-related information to help manage premium access, restore purchases, and validate purchase status.

The App does not use third-party advertising SDKs unless explicitly stated otherwise in a future update.

5. Personal Data

The developer does not operate a custom account system for ordinary use of the App and does not intentionally collect personal data through the App’s own backend during normal use.

However, if you use paid features, third-party providers such as Google Play and RevenueCat may process certain data as described above. Their processing is governed by their own privacy terms and policies.

6. Advertising

The App does not sell your personal data. The App does not use third-party advertising SDKs unless explicitly stated otherwise.

7. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or trade user data. Information may be processed by third-party providers only to the extent needed to operate app purchases, subscriptions, entitlement validation, purchase restoration, security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and related technical functions.

8. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device remains there until you remove it, clear the App’s data, or uninstall the App, subject to your device and system behavior.

Data processed by third-party providers such as Google Play and RevenueCat is retained according to their own policies and as required for billing, tax, accounting, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and technical operations.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intentionally directed to children under the age at which parental consent is required under applicable law. The developer does not knowingly collect personal data from children through a custom backend. If you believe that personal data relating to a child has been processed improperly, please contact the developer.
